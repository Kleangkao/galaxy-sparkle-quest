Guardians of Galia is an independent Alice Arcade browser game. It is not an
official Star Atlas product.

The project owner has confirmed permission to use the supplied Star Atlas visual
references and materials in this project. Artist/source links and the working art

approval screenshot to the private project records.

Third-party software notices remain available through the project dependencies
and their licenses.
