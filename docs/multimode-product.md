# Guardians of Galia — Multi-Mode Product System

## Shared promise

Players choose the kind of experience they want today without abandoning progress. Every mode uses the same captain rank, pilot, tool, companions, crystals, faction influence, and save profile.

## Current playable categories

### Story Expeditions

The ten-chapter hybrid campaign. Story missions combine grid exploration, hazards, objectives, rewards, companions, and faction influence.

### Swarm Protocol

A 75-second survival run inspired by survivor-style games:

- WASD or arrow-key movement
- automatic targeting and fire
- escalating enemy waves
- collectible energy drops
- level-up choices for damage, fire rate, speed, or pickup range
- a reusable pulse ability
- Ahr boss arrival and boss health tracking
- score, crystals, XP, and persistent high score

### Arcade Ops

Three handcrafted assignments: Ahr Blitz (boss assault), Crystal Rush (collection sprint), and Score Breaker (score attack). Each keeps its own best score and clear count. Combat now includes chasers, telegraphed dashers, orbiters, and Ahr radial hazard waves.

### Discovery Runs

A pressure-free search mode:

- no timer and no failure state
- three selectable biomes with rotating six-find field sets
- individual lore entries
- persistent discovery count
- persistent mastery ranks for each biome
- repeatable complete-the-journal objectives
- journal completion rewards

### Frontier Control

A strategy-lite command cycle:

- choose any of ten sectors
- spend four actions per cycle, with a fifth unlocked through PURI
- complete rotating secure, stabilize, or survey objectives
- read persistent sector traits that modify action values
- scout for a safe influence gain
- reinforce for a larger gain with rival reaction
- disrupt the leading rival while gaining a small foothold
- reach 100 influence to secure the sector
- earn objective bonuses and persistent command-cycle records
- outcomes persist into the campaign galaxy map

### Auto-pick

“Surprise me” chooses a rotating daily activity. It provides an instant option for younger players or anyone who does not want to compare modes.

## Product rules

1. No mode-specific currency.
2. No mode invalidates another mode’s progress.
3. Failure still awards a small amount of progress.
4. Controls remain visible on the play screen.
5. Modes are mechanically distinct, not difficulty labels.
6. New modes must use at least two shared systems such as pilots, tools, pets, faction influence, XP, or the codex.

## Shared progression additions

- PURI bond advances across combat, Discovery, and Strategy-lite sessions. Milestones at 10/25/50/75/100 unlock pickup range, bonus hull, Discovery guidance, an extra Strategy action, and a crystal multiplier.
- Combat pace and reduced-effects assists persist locally and never reduce rewards.
- Wide aim and high-contrast modes persist locally. Wide aim increases projectile and pulse forgiveness; high contrast strengthens borders, focus rings, threats, pickups, and player readability. Neither changes reward formulas.
- Combat uses one input layer for keyboard, pointer/touch controls, and standard gamepads. Movement is keyboard/WASD, an on-screen directional pad, or the left stick; pulse is Space, the UI button, or controller A.
- Game screens save through a profile repository contract. The current implementation remains local and offline-first, but can be replaced by an authenticated cloud repository without rewriting gameplay screens.
